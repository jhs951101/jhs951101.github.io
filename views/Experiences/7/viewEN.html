Background and Purpose
This is to show extraordinary passion.
Process to have Strengthened Abilities
I participate in anime concerts when they opens. When the concert started, we all stood up together and cheered passionately at the right time. If there was no cheering, I first cheered loudly and led the audience. Afterwards, after the concert, he always acted differently from others, such as shouting 'Good job!' to greet the audience. I am always doing this, and I am nurturing passion. Now, he is developing his passion by participating in DJ events where he can enjoy anime music.
Strengthened Abilities
  • Challenging spirit : Reinforced through the process of leading the audience
  • Passion, concentration : It has been strengthened through the process of cheering loudly.
Further Explanations
  • Animation Concert Live: This is an event that broadcasts live concerts held overseas (Japan) in movie theaters. Simply put, it's a live broadcast of an anime concert. Unlike a regular movie screening, you can cheer like a concert, swing a penlight, or stand up. In short, it's called 'live viewing'.
  • Animation DJ event: This is an event where you can enjoy while listening to anime music played by a DJ. Simply put, it is a DJ event where you can enjoy anime music. When you have fun, you can shout slogans, dance or just sit and listen. In short, it is called ‘Anisong Club’.
