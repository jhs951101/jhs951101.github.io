Background and Purpose
This is to make my own food and become independent.
Process to have Strengthened Abilities
I searched for the food I want to cook on 10,000 recipe sites and referenced the recipe. While referring to this recipe, I purchased all the necessary ingredients. After that, I watched the video at first and cooked it while following it. From then on, I tried to cook without looking as much as possible to memorize the recipe. When I had memorized almost all of the recipes like this, I studied my own recipe and cooked it myself, repeating the process of contemplation. After going through this process, I was able to provide my own delicious dishes to my family.
Strengthened Abilities
  • Effectiveness and problem-solving ability : It has been strengthened through the process of actually cooking.
  • Challenging spirit : It has been strengthened by continuously learning recipes and then cooking them.
  • Inquisition : It has been strengthened through the process of thinking about how to make delicious food.
  • Learning ability : Enhanced by learning new recipes.
Further Explanations
  • I usually find recipes on ten thousand recipe sites and cook them. Most representatively, I cooked rice balls, pork cutlet rice bowls, and agliolio pasta.
