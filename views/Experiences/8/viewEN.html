To do strength training at the gym

Background and Purpose
This is to strengthen the body and stamina to prevent lethargy and live a passionate life.
Process to have Strengthened Abilities
At first, I only used the treadmill to improve my stamina, but through the Internet, I found that aerobic exercise after strength training is effective. So, after researching the most effective strength training on YouTube, I reworked my workout plan. After that, I went to the gym and worked out as high as possible as much as I could endure. During this exercise, if any part of the body hurts, to overcome my limitations, I exercised a little more patience, then rested, and then exercised again. When I exercise like this, my body part is always sore and hard, but I feel like I am overcoming my limits and I do it every night.
Strengthened Abilities
  • Challenging spirit : It has been strengthened by starting a new exercise and increasing the intensity.
  • Patience : It has been strengthened through the process of exercising with utmost effort until a specific area hurts.
  • Stamina : It has been strengthened through the process of regular exercise.
Further Explanations
  • I work out at the gym for 2 hours every night, regardless of weekdays, weekends and holidays.
  • When you think of 'half easy and half difficult' while increasing the intensity, focus on that intensity.
