Process to have Strengthened Abilities
I researched how much the item I wanted to sell sold for in the past and offered a price. If we were contacted about items on sale, we responded as soon as possible. In particular, when asked a question, I answered it in as much detail as possible. Also, when I made a purchase, I gave my account number and deposit amount. When the deposit was confirmed, I immediately wrapped it in bubble wrap and sent it to the convenience store, and sent it after taking a picture of the waybill. At this time, I was very meticulous and kind enough to put the difference in the shipping cost in a separate courier. After going through this process, the number of sales exceeded 20.
