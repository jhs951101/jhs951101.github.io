To play a 3D rhythm game

Background and Purpose
This is to strengthen my own abilities and get rid of stress.
Process to have Strengthened Abilities
First, after receiving the tutorial, I repeated the work of increasing the level of the song and then playing it. While doing this, when I thought 'half easy and half difficult', I practiced the song intensively. If there is a part where I keep making mistakes, I thought about how to avoid making a mistake. If you keep practicing like this and your mistakes are reduced to 10% or less, raise the level of the song again and practice.
Strengthened Abilities
  • Agility : It has been strengthened through the process of touching the approaching stick at the right time.
  • Concentration : It has been strengthened through the process of concentration while playing the game.
Further Explanations
  • Rhythm game: A game where you touch an approaching stick to the beat of the music. The most representative example is the arcade game 'Pump It Up'.
  • There are various rhythm games such as arcade rhythm games and PC rhythm games, but I mainly play mobile rhythm games.
