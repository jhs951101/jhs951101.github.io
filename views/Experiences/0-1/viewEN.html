Background and Purpose
This is to increase the knowledges which is helpful for my life.
Process to have Strengthened Abilities
First of all, I read the book quickly to get a rough idea of what it was about. If you read it too slowly, you won't even be able to put it into action, so I read it as fast as I could. After reading it, I thought about how I could apply the knowledge I gained from the book to my actions. After that, I started to apply it when I run it directly. If I felt it was lacking, I slowly read only the parts I thought were necessary at that time.
Strengthened Abilities
  • Inquiry : It has been strengthened through the process of thinking about how to apply the acquired knowledge to everyday life.
  • Learning ability : Reinforced through the process of acquiring new knowledge.
